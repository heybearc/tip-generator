# What's New in Version 0.6.0

**Released:** April 22, 2026

---

## ✨ New Features

### Draft Collaboration

You can now invite other users to collaborate on your TIP drafts. Collaborators can view and edit the draft alongside you — useful for team reviews and co-authoring.

**What you can do:**
- Invite any user by searching their name or username
- See who is currently collaborating on a draft
- Remove collaborators at any time
- Shared drafts appear in your Drafts list with a "Shared" badge

**How to use it:**
1. Open any draft you own
2. Click the **👥 (Users)** button in the top-right action bar
3. Type a name or username in the search field — suggestions appear as you type
4. Click a suggestion and hit **Invite**
5. The collaborator can now access and edit the draft from their own Drafts page

**Permission rules:**
- Only the draft **owner** can invite or remove collaborators
- Collaborators can read and edit — but cannot delete the draft
- Admins can manage collaborators on any draft

---

### Documents Shared Across Team

All uploaded documents (discovery docs, service orders) are now visible to everyone on the team, not just the person who uploaded them. This makes it easier to generate TIPs from documents uploaded by colleagues.

---

## 💡 What You Need to Know

- **Shared drafts** show a purple **Shared** badge in your Drafts list
- **Owned drafts** with collaborators show a collaborator count badge
- Rename and delete buttons only appear for draft owners and admins
- The collaborator search requires at least 2 characters to show results
