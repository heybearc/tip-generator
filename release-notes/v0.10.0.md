# What's New in Version 0.10.0

**Released:** April 23, 2026

---

## ✨ New Features

### Section Order & Visibility Management
You can now control exactly which sections appear in your exported TIP — and in what order.

**What you can do:**
- Show or hide individual sections before exporting
- Drag and drop sections to reorder them
- Changes are saved per draft and reflected in the Word/PDF export

**How to use it:**
1. Open a completed draft
2. Click the **Manage Sections** button at the top of the draft view
3. Toggle sections on/off or drag to reorder
4. Export — only visible sections are included

---

### Complete Pillar Generation (All 6 Pillars)
The "Approximate Timing" section now reliably generates all 6 workstream pillars in full detail. Previously, generation would sometimes stop after 2 pillars due to token limits.

**What changed:**
- Each pair of pillars is now generated in its own dedicated pass
- No content is cut off or missing
- The progress counter reflects the true number of generation passes

---

## 🐛 Bug Fixes

### PII Scrubbing Now Works Correctly
The opt-in PII scrubbing feature (available on the Generate page) was silently failing due to an internal conflict between the scrubbing process and the database. This is now fixed — PII is properly replaced with tokens before being sent to Claude and restored in the final draft.

### Generation Progress Shows Correct Count
The progress bar was showing "4 batches" even for large documents that required more passes. It now accurately reflects the actual number of generation passes in real time.

### Cancel Generation Works Reliably
Cancelling a generation now immediately stops the process at the next checkpoint and clears the "Starting generation..." spinner. Previously, a cancelled draft could appear stuck.

### Refine All Sections No Longer Times Out
The "Whole-Document Refine" feature (apply an instruction to all sections at once) was failing on large drafts because it was trying to process too many sections in one request. Structural placeholder sections are now skipped, and processing is faster.

---

## 🔧 Infrastructure

### Environment-Isolated Celery Workers
The blue/green deployment environments now each have their own dedicated background worker. Tasks from the live environment are processed by the live worker, and tasks from standby are processed by the standby worker — no cross-contamination.

---

## 💡 What You Need to Know

- No action required — all changes are automatic
- If you had a draft stuck in "generating" state, it has been cleared
- The Section Manager panel is available on all completed drafts
- PII scrubbing toggle is on the Generate page — enable it before starting generation
