# What's New in Version 0.7.0

**Released:** April 22, 2026

---

## ✨ New Features

### Whole-Document Refine
A new **Whole-Document Refine** panel now appears at the top of every draft's section list. Give it a single free-text instruction and Claude will apply it to every section simultaneously — in parallel.

**What you can do:**
- Standardize formatting across the entire document in one step
- Convert all bullet lists to numbered steps throughout
- Replace placeholder text like `[DATA NEEDED]` with `TBD` everywhere
- Apply any global instruction — the input is completely free-form

**How to use it:**
1. Open any completed draft
2. Click **Whole-Document Refine** at the top of the section list to expand it
3. Type your instruction in the text box
4. Click **Run on All Sections**
5. Review the section-by-section preview
6. Click **Apply All** to save the changes across the entire document

---

### Custom Mode in Per-Section Refine
The per-section **Refine** panel (the ✨ button on each section) now includes a **Custom** tab alongside Tighten, Apply Template, Fix Risks Format, and Tighten + Apply.

**What you can do:**
- Write any free-form instruction targeted at a single section
- Make small, precise edits without affecting the rest of the document
- Combine the precision of section-level editing with the flexibility of natural language

**How to use it:**
1. Expand a section and click **Refine**
2. Click the **Custom** tab
3. Type your instruction (e.g. "Add a bullet point about change management")
4. Press **Enter** or click **Run**
5. Review the suggestion and apply or discard

## 🐛 Bug Fixes

### AI Assist — Suggestions Were Rewriting the Entire Document
The default AI Assist suggestions (like "Make the executive summary more concise") were incorrectly sending the entire document as context, causing Claude to rewrite the whole draft. Suggestions are now whole-document in scope, and section-specific work is properly directed to the **Refine** button.

### AI Assist — Copy Suggestion Button Not Working
The **Copy suggestion** button in the AI Assist chat panel was silently doing nothing when no section was pinned. It now correctly copies the suggestion to your clipboard.

### AI Assist — 400 Error on Section-Based Drafts
Opening AI Assist on a draft that uses the sections structure (all recently generated drafts) was returning a confusing error. This has been fixed — context is now correctly gathered from all sections.

---

## 💡 What You Need to Know

- No action required — all changes are immediately available
- The **Whole-Document Refine** panel only appears on drafts that have sections
- Whole-document refinements are previewed before applying — nothing is saved until you click **Apply All**
- Section-level **Custom** mode uses the same AI model and safety rules as all other Refine modes
