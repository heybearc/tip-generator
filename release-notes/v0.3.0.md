# What's New in Version 0.3.0

**Released:** 2026-04-21

---

## 🔐 Secure Login

TIP Generator now requires you to sign in before accessing any features.

### Sign In with Authentik
All access is now protected by Thrive's Authentik identity provider. When you visit the app, you'll be taken to a sign-in page where you can authenticate using your Cloudigan account.

**What's new:**
- Secure login screen at every entry point
- Your name appears in the top-right corner of the navigation bar
- One-click sign out from any page
- Sessions last 60 minutes, then you'll be asked to sign in again

### Your Content is Private
All documents, drafts, and generated TIPs are now associated with your account. Only you can see and access the content you've uploaded or generated.

---

## 💡 What You Need to Know

- **First time visiting?** You'll be redirected to the sign-in page automatically
- **Already using the app?** Your existing drafts and documents are still there — they've been migrated to the admin account
- **Sign out** using the logout button (arrow icon) in the top-right navigation bar
